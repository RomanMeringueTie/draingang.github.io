History

The group formed in 2013 in Stockholm Thaiboy Digital was residing in Sweden at the time. Bladee and Ecco2K had previously collaborated as the grindcore duo Krossad. Signing to the YEAR0001 label, the members of Drain Gang gained prominence in the cloud rap scene from their collaborations with labelmate Yung Lean.

Latest Releases

Spiderr is the eighth studio album by Swedish musician Bladee. It was released on 30 September 2022 via Year0001. Full album produced by Whitearmor.

Crest is a collaborative studio album from Swedish musicians Bladee and Ecco2K. It was released on 17 March 2022 via Year0001, as a follow-up to the duo's previous single "Amygdala".The album was "recorded amongst nature", with Bladee, Ecco2K, and producer Whitearmor driving to the edge of Sweden and recording in a cabin not far from where Ingmar Bergman's fantasy epic The Seventh Seal was filmed.

Trash Island is a collaborative studio album by Swedish collective Drain Gang, released on 12 September 2019 through Year0001. It is sung by Bladee, Ecco2K and Thaiboy Digital, and all songs were produced by Whitearmor, with "Acid Rain" and "You Lose" also being produced by Mechatok and Lusi, respectively. The song "1:1" features Yung Lean. It was recorded in a visit to Bangkok, Thailand, as Thaiboy Digital was deported from Stockholm in 2015 due to visa complications. The artwork was done by Ecco2K.

Whitearmor's latest album, consisting of 10 tracks, is not just a compilation of good songs. It is a musical journey that is purposefully put together, showcasing Whitearmor's distinct production style and aesthetic. As a fan of Whitearmor, I find that this album lives up to the high standard set by his previous works and stands out as the only Whitearmor album as of 2023. The standout features of this album are all within it's production. The album's production is reminiscent of Whitearmor's previous works like Crest and 333, while also incorporating his iconic sound selection. It deviates from Whitearmor's earlier work as Dj Cannabiz, which was inspired by Clams Casino, and highlights Whitearmor's uniqueness as a producer. The album takes a departure from the clams casino inspired tape that marked the beginning of his career, and we are able to witness his evolution as an artist.
